<b><h2>BetPlugin</h2></b>

Hi there, i wanna introduce you my first Minecraft 1.19.3 plugin: BetPlugin. 

Use: /bet
You have 45% of chance of duplicate the amount of the item that you have in your hand, or lose everything away.
